ARRIVAL GUIDE

Bali Airport — What You Need to Know

Ngurah Rai International Airport

IATA: DPS · 13 km from Kuta · 25 km from Ubud

Best for international arrivals

The only airport serving Bali. Domestic and international terminals are separate buildings connected by a short walk. Garuda Indonesia, Lion Air, Singapore Airlines, and AirAsia are among the main carriers here. Arrivals can be slow during peak periods — allow extra time at immigration.

TransportCost (approx)Duration

Check-in tips

  • ✓Online check-in opens 24-48 hours before departure — complete it to avoid airport queues during peak season.
  • ✓The domestic terminal (Terminal 2) is a separate building — confirm your terminal before heading to check-in.
  • ✓Visa on Arrival booths are before passport control — pay IDR 500,000 (approx $30) or use the VoA e-form online before travel.

Frequently Asked Questions about Flights to Bali

Everything you need to know before booking your Bali flight on FlyFairly.

Garuda Indonesia, Lion Air, Citilink, AirAsia, Singapore Airlines, Scoot, Qantas, and Jetstar operate direct flights to Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS). Direct routes are available from Jakarta, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, and Tokyo, among other cities.
February is typically the cheapest month to fly to Bali. The wet season reduces demand and fares can be 30–40% lower than the July–August peak. January and November are also budget-friendly. Book early for June, July, August, and December when fares spike significantly.
Yes. FlyFairly supports Atome, Kredivo, and GrabPay Later at checkout for all Bali flights. Select your provider after choosing your flight, pick your instalment plan, and complete the approval process. Your e-ticket is delivered immediately after booking confirmation.
Atome and GrabPay Later do not perform hard credit checks for their standard plans — so using them has no impact on your credit score. Kredivo uses a standard credit approval process. Missed payments on any BNPL provider may affect your credit profile. Check each provider's terms before applying.
Indonesia offers visa-free entry or a Visa on Arrival (VoA) for nationals of many countries including Singapore, Malaysia, Australia, the UK, USA, and most EU nations. The VoA costs IDR 500,000 (approximately $30 USD) and is valid for 30 days, extendable once. Always verify current entry requirements at imigrasi.go.id before travelling.
Bali is served by Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS), located about 13 km south of Kuta and 25 km from Ubud. Metered taxis and ride-hailing apps (Grab, Gojek) are the most practical options. A metered taxi to Seminyak costs approximately IDR 100,000–150,000. Avoid unlicensed taxi touts inside the terminal.
The dry season from April to October is generally the best time to visit Bali. May, June, and September are the sweet spot — excellent weather, lower crowds than July and August, and more competitive fares. July and August have the best weather but the highest prices and tourist density. The wet season (November–March) has lower prices and fewer crowds, but expect afternoon showers.
