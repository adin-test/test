Flight status explained

WHAT DOES
IT MEAN?

On Schedule

Flight is scheduled and will depart on time. All systems go.

Delayed

Departure is pushed back. Keep checking for updates.

Departed

Flight has left the gate and is about to take off.

In Air

Flight is en route. Live position updating now.

Landed

Flight has touched down at the destination airport.

Arrived

Flight has pulled into the gate. Bags will follow shortly.

Canceled

This flight has been canceled. Contact your airline immediately.

Unknown

Status not available right now. Try again in a few minutes.
