Our take on Klarna

Klarna earns a strong rating as the most globally accessible BNPL provider on Laters. Its multi-plan structure — Pay in 4, Pay in 30, and Financing / Pay Monthly — makes it versatile across both short-haul budget bookings and long-haul Business Class fares. Approval is near-instant for Pay in 4 and Pay in 30, with no hard credit check.

Where Klarna leads

Global reach is Klarna's clearest advantage. Available in 45+ countries, it serves travellers from the US, UK, Australia, Germany, and across Europe far better than Southeast Asia-focused alternatives. The Pay in 30 plan is uniquely strong for flight bookings — it lets you lock in a fare without spending anything upfront until the following month.

Eligibility & limits

Klarna requires a linked debit or credit card. Pay in 4 and Pay in 30 do not require a credit check. Financing / Pay Monthly plans require a soft credit check; APR ranges from 0.00%–35.99% depending on creditworthiness. Spending limits start lower for new users and increase after consistent on-time payments.

About Buy Now Pay Later Flights with Klarna

Klarna is a Swedish financial technology company founded in 2005 and one of the world's most widely used Buy Now Pay Later providers. It operates across 45+ countries and serves over 150 million consumers globally. On Laters, Klarna is available at checkout for every flight booking — from economy seats on low-cost carriers to business class fares on full-service airlines.

The most popular Klarna plan for flight bookings is Pay in 4, which splits your total fare into four equal biweekly payments with 0% interest. 25% is charged upfront at checkout; the remaining three payments follow automatically every 14 days. Pay in 4 is available on baskets $35–$1,000 — late fees up to $7 may apply for missed payments. For travellers who prefer to pay nothing upfront, Pay in 30 allows you to defer your full balance for 30 days at no interest — ideal for locking in a fare before your next paycheque.

For long-haul routes and premium cabin bookings where fares exceed the $1,000 Pay in 4 basket limit, Klarna's Financing / Pay Monthly plan is the most flexible option. Available in 6, 12, 18, or 24-month terms, it spreads even a $2,000 Business Class fare into manageable monthly payments. APR ranges from 0.00%–35.99% depending on your credit profile, and is displayed clearly before you confirm. A soft credit check applies and does not affect your credit score.

Responsible borrowing: Klarna is a form of credit. Only use Buy Now Pay Later for flights you can genuinely afford to repay. Missing Pay in 4 payments may incur late fees up to $7. See Klarna's full terms at klarna.com.
